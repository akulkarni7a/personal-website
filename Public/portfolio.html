Recruitment


Overview

The Recruitment app was designed to screen potential teaching candidates. It asks candidates various basic information gathering questions as well as more indepth industry specific questions. Returned responses are dynamic based on user input.

At the end, key data points are emailed to a specified email account.

*This app is property of Anirudh Kulkarni. References to Endeavor Schools was used with permission
Steps

  1. Text +1 (214) 491-5697 as if you were looking for a job
  2. Ex: "I'm looking for a job"
  3. Follow prompts
  4. If the app asks you to list previous employer, list a nationally recognized franchise

Retention


Overview

The Retention app was designed to do quick Net Promoter Score (NPS) Surveys. Responses are dynamic based on user input. All user responses are then forwarded to a specified email.

*This app is property of Anirudh Kulkarni. References to Endeavor Schools was used with permission
Steps

  1. Normally, the user would input a list of phone numbers separated by commas in the terminal
  2. Text +1 (972) 544-4597 as if you were responding to "How likely are you to refer a friend or family to our school?" question
  3. Ex: "9"
